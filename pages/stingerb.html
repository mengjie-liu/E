Homage to Parking Lot. Dumpster and Poricy Pond
next project

found steel, rusted scaffolding, concrete, old tire, found and altered sign, etched aluminum composite panel, driveway sealant, sediment, paint, found wood, construction adhesives. Dimensions variable.
2021

This installation is inspired by the feeling of “psychic or existential distress caused by environmental change” called solastalgia. Much of the material in this piece was collected from my home in New Jersey, where detritus settles, in the interstitial space between the roadways and the woods.
