Calling All Conifers
next project

Pine trees, steel, various adhesives, wires, amplifier, speakers, modified AM radio static (10:02), MP3 player. Dimensions variable.
2020

Two antler-like wooden “earbuds” drop from the ceiling and bellow unidentifiable sounds, inviting the viewer to stand between them. These antennae or receivers are meant to send a transmission signal connecting humans with trees.
